Jjangu Yesu Obe Mu Nze
Come Thou Fount
An
3
4
A.Nettleton
Doh is Eb 
1. Jjangu Yesu obe mu nze, Lungamy(a) omwoyo gwange,
Guyimbeng(a) ebiro byonna, Okukutendereza.
Njigi-riza okusi-nza, Ndage-ng(a) obulungi bwo,
Essuubi ly'amaka gaffe nga linjijuz(a) essanyu.

2. Nyimus(a) eddoboozi lyange, Nga nsab(a) okuyamba kwo,
Olwokwagala kw(o) okungi, Olintuusa mu ggulu;
Yesu- (O)mununuzi wa-nge, Ggwe (e)ya-mponya okufa,
Noggyaw(o) ebyonono byange, Bwewawangul(a) okufa.

3. Yesu wang(e) okwagala kwo, Bulijjo kweyongere
Olwekisa kyo ekingi, Kanywererenga ku ggwe.
Era- nnem(e) okukuva-ko, Mbeere mu kwagala kwo,
Twala obulamu bwange, Butegeker(e) eggulu.
