Yesu Atuyita N'ekisa Ekingi
Softly and Tenderly
6
8
Will L Thompson
Doh is Ab 
1. Yesu ayita n'ekisa ekingi, Ggwe nange atuyita;
Ayimiridde nga alindirira, Alinze ggw(e) era nange.

Mujje, eka!- Mwenna abakooy(e) ennyo!-
Yes(u) abayita n'ekisa ekingi,
Komawo mangu - - - e - - - ka.

2. Yesu yegayirira tunagaana? Yegayirira fenna,
Lwaki tugaana ekisa kye kino, Ky'atulaze ffe fenna?

3. Ekiseera ky'ekisa kye kiyita, Olinze kuggalirwa?
Esaawa eyokufa esembera, Esemberedde ffenna.

4. Lowooza okusuubiza kwe kwonna, Kw'asuubiza ggwe nange:
Ffe twayonoona naye asonyiye, Atusonyiye fenna.
