1. Yesu atuyita fenna, Tukole mu nnimiro;
Kubanga zonna zengedde, Anakola aluwa?
Ayita ggwe era nange, Empeera ye nnene nnyo;
Ani addamu n'essanyu, Nti "Nze nzuno, ontume."

2. Ne bw'otogenda wala nnyo kunoonya ababula,
Ojja kubasanga bangi, kumpi ku miriraano,
Oba toli mwogezi nnyo, Okwenkana Paulo,
Yogera ku Yesu kyokka, Nga yafa kulwa bonna.
