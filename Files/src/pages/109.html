Buli Muntu Yenna Awulire
Whosoever Heareth

P P Bliss
Doh is D 
1.Buli muntu yenna awulire Ebigambo by'Omulokozi w'ensi,
Bunya wonna wonna nti akkiriza, Alirokoka - - - mu ye.

Buli awulira, ekigambo kye
Ategez(e) abalala mu nsi yonna,
Kitaffe ayita - Komawo eka
Bul(i) awulidde - - - jjangu.

2.Buli muntu yenna asembere, Oluggi (o)lw'omu ggulu lugguddwawo,
Yesu ge mazima, lye Kkubo lyaffe, Ekibalwisa - - - kiki?

3.Buli muntu yenna ayagala, Ekigambo kya Katond(a) atalimba;
Kale bannange, tubuny(e) ettendo lye, Atugabidde - - - buwa.
