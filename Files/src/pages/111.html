Omusawo ali Kumpi
The Great Physician Now Is Near

Arr by J H Stockton
Doh is Ab 
1. Omusawo ali kumpi, - - Mukama ow'ekisa;
Azzaam(u) amaany(i) abakooye,- Wulir(a) eddoboozi lye.

A-sinzibwa muggulu, N'a-sinzibwa ne ku nsi;
Kirungi okuyimba, Ku linnya lye Yesu.

2. Ayi Omwana gw'endiga, - - Nzikiriza (o)kufa kwo;
Nsanyukira erinnya lyo, - - Ery'omuwendo mungi.

3. Erinnya lye lindokola, - - Erinnya lye lyokka,
Nsanyuka okuwulira, - - Yesu nga asinzibwa.

4. Bwalikomawo n'engule, - - Engule ey'obulamu;
Tulituula wamu naye, - - Twekulise ens(i) eno.
