CH 341
Ggwe Okkoye Onafuwadde
Art Thou Weary, Art Thou Languid?
4
4
Henry W Baker
Doh is G 
1. Ozitowereddw(a) okooye, onafuwadde?
Yes(u) a-gamba Genda- gy'al(i) owummule!

2. Alina bubonero ki obumundaga?
(E)nkovu- mu bibatu- ne ku bigere.

3. (O)mutwe gwe gulik(o) engule y'obwakabaka?
Guli-ko engule,- naye ya maggwa.

4. Nz(e) okumugoberera nfunamu kiki?
Oku-kola n'ama-ziga, n'ennaku.

5. Bwemmulemerako Yesu edd(a) alimpa ki?
Oliwangul(a) amagombe n'okufa.

6. Bwemmusab(a) okunsembeza tangobe waali?
Oku-goba, nedda,- nedda, tayinza.

7. Bwe ng'umira byonna byonn(a) alimp(a) omukisa?
Aba-tukuvu ba-gamba - Wewaawo!
