CH 530
Waliwo Ensulo Ey'omusaayi
There is a Fountain Filled with Blood
An
4
4
Lowell Mason
Doh is C 
1. Wa-liw(o) ensul(o) ey'omusaayi, O-kuva- mu- Yesu;
A-bonoonyi banaabamu, Ne-bawon(a) ekibi.
Ne-bawon(a) ekibi- Nebawon(a) e-ki-bi,
A-bonoonyi banaabamu, Ne-bawon(a) ekibi.

2. (O)mu-bbi oli yasanyuka (O)ku-lab(a) ensul(o) e-yo;
Na-nge omwonoonyi ennyo, Nnaa-tukuzibw(a) omwo.
Nnaa-tukuzibw(a) omwo -- Nnaatukuziwbw(a) o-mwo;
Na-nge omwonoonyi ennyo, Nnaa-tukuzibw(a) omwo.

3. A-yi Omwana gw'Endiga, (O)mu-saayi gwo gu-kola;
(O)ku-tuusa ekkanisa yo, Lwe-linunulibwa.
Lwe-linunulibwa - Lwelinunu-li-bwa;
(O)ku-tuusa ekkanisa yo, Lwe-linunulibwa.

4. (O)ku-va lwennalab(a) ensulo, E-y'ebiwundu byo;
Ndo-wooza ku kwagala kwo, (O)ku-tuusa lwe ndifa.
(O)ku-tuusa lwe ndifa (O)kutuusa lwe ndi-fa;
Ndo-wooza ku kwagala kwo, (O)ku-uusa lwe ndifa.

5. Kululi lwendizuukira, O-kuva mu ntaana,
A-maanyi g(o) agalokola, Ge-ndiyimbangako.
Ge-ndiyimbangako - Gendiyimba-nga-ko,
A-maanyi g(o) agalokola, Ge-ndiyimbangako.
