Yesu Ang'amba
I Hear the Saviour Say

Doh is Eb 
1. Yesu a'ngamba nti, "Ggwe tolina maanyi;
Tunulanga osabe, Nze nnaakuwanga byonna."

Yansasulira Ebbanja lyonna,
Nayonoonebw(a) ekibi, - - -
Ye nantukuza nze.

2. A-maanyi go gokka, Gegawony(a) ekibi;
Gakyusa n'omutima, Omugumu ng'ejjinja.

3. Si-rina kalungi, Kasaanira kisa;
Ka njoz(e) engoye zange, Mu musaayi gwo Yesu.

4. Bwe-ndiyimirira, Mu maaso g'entebe;
Nditeek(a) engule yange, Ku bigere byo Yesu.
