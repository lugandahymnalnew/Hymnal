Awo Ku Musaaalaba
Jesus, Keep Me Near the Cross
6

Wm H Doane
Doh is F 
1. Awo ku musalaba, Awali amazzi
Aganaaza ebibi, W'oba onkuumira.

Ku musaalaba gwe, Kwe nenyumiriza,
(O)kutuusa nga mpumudde, Muggulu ewaffe.

2. Okwo nze omunaku, Kwe nsanga eddembe;
Omusana gwa Yesu, Ne gunjakira nze.

3. Kwe nsanga Yesu wange, Ne yeeraga gyendi;
Nga ampa amaanyi ge, Gabeeranga mu nze.

4. Awo we nnabeeranga, Nga neesiga Yesu;
Alyoke annongose, Ye antuuse gy'ali.
