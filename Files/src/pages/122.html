Newaddeyo Gy'oli Yesu
All to Jesus I Surrender
4
4
W S Weeden
Doh is D 
1. Newaddeyo gy'oli Yesu, Newaddeyo nze nzenna;
Nkwagala era nkwesiga, W'oli we nnabeeranga.

Newayo gy'oli, Newaayo gy'oli,
Newaddeyo gy'oli Yesu, Newaayo gy'oli.

2. Newaddeyo gy'oli Yesu, Ndi wano mu maaso go;
Nziyako ebye'nsi byonna, Onfuule omwana wo.

3. Newaddeyo gy'oli Yesu, Onnaze ontukuze;
Omwoyo wo abe nange, Ntegeere nga ndi wuwo.

4. Newaddeyo gy'oli Yesu, Mpulira amaanyi go,
N'essanyu ly'obulokozi, Nkwebaza nnyo Mukama.
