Olwo'okukkiriza Nnyinza
My Faith Looks Up to Thee
4
4
Lowell Mason
Doh is Eb 
1. Olw'okukkiriza, Nyinz(a) okulaba Ggwe (O)mulokozi,
Wulira nga nsaba, Ggyaw(o) ebibi byange,
Okuva kakano mbeere wuwo.

2. Ekisa kyo Yesu, Kimpa amaanyi go Ndiwangula,
Nga bwewanfiirira, Kkiriza kye nkuwa
Obulamu bwange Bukozese.

3. Onyambe, Mukama, Olw'okutegeera (E)kigambo kyo;
(O)mwoyo w(o) annu'ngamye Nange nnaasomanga,
Ndyoke nkutegeere Mu kitabo.

4. Wano ndi mu kabi N'olw'ekyo sembera Kumpi nange;
(O)nkwata ku mukono Nnem(e) okukankana
Ontwale ewuwo, Ng'okomyewo!
