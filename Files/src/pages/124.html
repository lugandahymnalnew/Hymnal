Okkoye Nnyo?
Are You Weary [Tell It to Jesus]
4
4
E S Lorenz
Doh is Bb 
1. Okooye nnyo, Ozitowereddwa? Tegeeza Yesu, tegeeza Yesu;
Olin(a) ennaku nnyingi ku mwoyo? Gend(a) eri Yesu totya

Tegeeza Yesu, tegeeza Yesu, Ye mukwano gwo ddala;
Tewali mulala amusinga, Gend(a) eri Yesu totya.

2. Okulukus(a) amaziga mangi? Tegeeza Yesu, tegeeza Yesu.
Olin(a) ebib(i) ebitamanyiddwa, Gend(a) eri Yesu totya.

3. Otidde nnyo olw'entiisa egya? Tegeeza Yesu, tegeeza Yesu.
Weralikiridd(e) olw'okujja kwe, Genda kaakano gy'ali.

4. Obang(a) oyagala emirembe, Tegeeza Yesu, tegeeza Yesu;
Olyoke osanyuke lw'alidda, Genda kaakano gyali.
