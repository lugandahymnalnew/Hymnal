SDAH 530
Bwenfuna Eddembe
When Peace Like river
An
4
4
P P Bliss
Doh is Db 
1. Bwe nfun(a) eddembe mu bula-mu bwange, Oba bwe mbeera mu nnaku.
Onjigirize okukkirizanga, Buli mbeera yonna mwontadde.

Mirembe, Mirembe; Mukama njagala mirembe.

2. Setani bw'aleeta ebi-kemo bye, Yimusa essuubi lyange,
Nti Yesu alab(a) obunafu bwange, Era Ye atambula nange.

3. (E)kigambo kino kyokka kye- neesiga, Nti ebibi byange byonna,
Byakomererwa ku musaalaba gwe, Mukama Yesu yeebazibwe.

4. Mukama ayanguye o-lunaku, Olulibikkula byonna;
(E)ssuubi liritukirira lwalidda, Ndimulaba Yesu n'amaaso.
