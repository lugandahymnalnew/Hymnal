1. Yesu wasuubiza ffe abantu bo; Bwe tubeer(a) awamu, oba kumpi nnyo
Kigambo-kakano tukikkiriza, Beera naffe wano, otusanyuse

Yesu, jja-ngu muffe, Nga tuli- wano;

2. Bullijo Mukama obadde naffe, Yongera kubeera awamu wano;
Ggwe totu-vangako tukwetaaga nnyo, Ayi Yesu jjangu, tukulabeko.

(E)ssuubi lya-fe mu ggwe lyeyongerenga, Naffe tweyonger(e) okukufaanana.
