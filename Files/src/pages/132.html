1. Muka-ma Ye Mu-sumba se-tagenga,-
Anga-lamiza- mu muddo-- omuto;
Antwa-l(a) awali- oluzz(i) oluteefu,
Ako-myaw(o) emme-me yange--munteeko.

2. Annu'-ngamya mu- butuuki-rivu bwe,-
Newa-kubadde- nga mbeera-- mu kufa;
Nga Ggw(e) o-li nange- seraliikirira,
(O)luga- lwo n'omu-ggo gwo bye-- binkuuma.

3. (A)bala-be ne bwe- babawo- ondiisa,-
Onsii-ze ama-futago--ku mutwe;
(E)kiko-mpe kyange- kijjud(e) omubisi
Nze na-beeranga- ne Yesu-- bulijjo.

4. (O)bulu-ngi bwo n'e-kisa kyo Kitange,-
Kaba-ka era- Katonda-- wange nze;
Bibee-renga ku- nze buli gye mbeera,
Oku-tuusa lw'o-limpa e--ngule
