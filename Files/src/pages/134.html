1. Waayo ekiseera eri Katonda;
Nyumyanga ne Yesu mu Kigambo kye,
Kwananga ababe, Yamb(a) abanafu
Noonyang(a) omukisa gw'oyo bulijjo.

2. Waayo ekiseera eri Katonda;
Beeranga mu kyama wamu ne Yesu.
Olw'okumulaba ofuuke nga ye,
Ne mu mpisa zo nga olaga Yesu.

3. Waayo ekiseera eri Katonda,
Era linda ye akukulembere,
Mu ssanyu, mu nnaku, beeranga naye,
Tunuulira Yesu nâ€™Ekigambo kye.

4. Waayo ekiseera eri Katonda;
Obeere n'eddembe mu mutima gwo.
Bw'okulemberwa Omwoyo we yekka,
Akusanyize okumuwereza.
