CH 416
Kwata Omukono Gwange
Hold Thou My Hand
4
4

Doh is Eb 
1. Kwat(a) omukono gwange, ndi munafu,
Siyinza nze ku tambula nzekka;
Naye bw'onyweza siriko kye nnaatya,
Nawangula byonna ebintiisa.

2. (O) mulokozi, kwat(a) omukono gwange,
Onsembeze kumpi n'Omwoyo gwo;
Enzikiz(a) ekutte, onjakire nze,
Nem(e) okuva mu kkubo ly(o) eddungi.

3. Kwat(a) omukono gwange, mu bulwadde,
Nga nzirika nga nnaater(a) okufa;
Bw'obeera nange nnaafun(a) emirembe,
Nnaatambula n'essanyu lingi nnyo.
