Just as I Am

1. Mu-kama, nze- nin(a) e-bibi, Naye- ekisa kyo kingi;
O-mpise o-kujja gy'oli, -
Nz'omwonoonyi,-  Yesu nzijja.

2. Ye-su, Omwa-na gw'e-ndiga, Olw'e-bibi byange wafa,
(O)Lw'o-musaayi gwo- nkukkiriza,-
Nz'omwonoonyi,- Yesu nzijja.

3. "A-jja gyendi- simu-goba," (E) kiga-mbo kyo nkikkiriza,
Ku-b(a) okuli-mba toyinza,-
Nz'omwonoonyi,- Yesu nzijja.

4. O-kwagala- kw(o) oku-nene N'eki-sa kyo bimpaludde;
Kye- njagala,- kusenga Ggwe,-
Nz'omwonoonyi,- Yesu nzijja.
