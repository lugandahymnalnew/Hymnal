Wansi W'omusalaba
Beneath the Cross of Jesus
4
4
Frederick C Maker
Doh is Db 
1. Wansi w'omusalaba We nyimirira nze 
Guli ng(a) ekisikirize Ky'olwazi mu ddungu;
Era guli ng(a) ekisulo Mu lukool(a) olubi,
Abatambuz(e) abanaku We bawummulira.

2. We guli we mpummuza Omugugu gwange,
Omusana nga gwaka nnyo, Newogoma mu gwo;
Bwe ngufumitiriza ko, Ndowooza nnyo Yesu
Eyafa ku musalaba, Mmufukaamirira.

3. Yesu, Omusana omulala, saagala,
Amaaso go galabika Gyendi nga enjuba; mo
Amadaala agagenda Mu ggulu ge gano;
Omusalaba gwo, Yesu, Era gwe gwange nze.

4. (E)byekitalo bibiri, Bye njatula leero:
(E)bibi byange bingi naye (O)kwagala kwo kungi;
(O)musaalaba gunsanyusa Okusinga byonna,
Ensi leka empiteko, Ndaby(e) ekitiibwa kyo!
