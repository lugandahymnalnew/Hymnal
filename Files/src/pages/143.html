CH 670
Kitange Nze Nkukowoola
Father' I Stretch My Hands
An
3
4
Arranged
Doh is G 
1. Kitange, nze nkukowoola, Ggwe Mubeezi wange;
To-nzigyako ku-kuuma kwo, Ne-me okubu-la.

2. (O)mugabi w'okukkiriza, nze nkutunulidde;
E-kirabo kyo- nkyetaaga, we- kitali nfa- nze.

3. Mmanyi nga tondeke kufa, Yogera, omponye;
Ka-kano ka nni-ndirire, O-mwoyo (o)mubee-zi.

4. Ndijjula essanyu lingi, Nga ndab(a) amaaso go;
Mpuliza eki-gambo kyo, Mpee-bwe n'ekisa- kyo.

5. Nzikiriza ddala Yesu, Nga wanfirira nze;
Noo-yiwa omu-saayi gwo, Nso-nyiyibw(e) ebi-bi.
