Ompise Yesu
I am Thine, O Lord

Doh is Ab 
1. Ompise, Yesu Mukama wange, N'eddoboozi (e)ry'eki-sa;
Naye njagala okusemberera, Kumpi nnyo naawe, gy'oli!

Onsembe - ze, Kumpi kumpi nnyo,
Nsembeza gy'oli Yesu;
Onsembe - ze, Kumpi, kumpi nnyo, - - -
Awo ku musaalaba.

2. Fuka omwoyo wo ow'ekisa Ku nze nkuweereze-nga;
(O)mutima gwange gubeere ku Ggwe, Nkwagalenga Ggwe wekka.

3. Okwagala kwo kwa buziba nnyo, Ndikutegeera ntuu-se;
Kutumbira waggulu ewala, Gye siyinza kulaba.
