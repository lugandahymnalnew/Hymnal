Oyagala aaobe Wa Yesu?
Would You Live for Jesus?
4
4
J H Tenney
Doh is G 
1. Oyagal(a) obe wa Yesu akulongose? Wetaag(a) otambule mu kubbo lye? 
Oyagala yetikkenga obuzibu bwo? Mugany(e) akulu'ngamye!

Amaanyi ge ganakulongoosa; (O)musaayi gwe gunaakutukuza; 
Bw'anakujjuza okwagala kwe, Onosanyuka ng'akulu'ngamya! 

2. Oyagala akutuuse ku ddembe ddala? Osobole okumugoberera?
Oyagala onywezebwe mu mikono gye? Mugany(e) akulu'ngamye!

3. Oyagala akutuuse mu kiwummulo? Oyagala obeer(e) omuwanguzi
Era oleetenga bangi abakungulwa? Mugany(e) akulu'ngamye!
