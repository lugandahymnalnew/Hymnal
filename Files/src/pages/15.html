Doh is Bb 
1. Yesu ali-fuga- wonna, Wonn(a) omusana - we gwaka:
Obwakabaka bwe- bu-fuge, (O)kutuusa e-nnaku- zonna

2. Abantu be-nnimi- zonna, Batenderez(e) e-kisa kye;
Abaana banabuu-li-ranga, Obulungi- bweri-nnya lye,

3. Omukisa-gwe we-guli, (A)basibe bafu n(e) eddembe;
Abakooye bana-wu-mmula, Abetaaga- ne ba-kkuta.

4. Buli muntu- yimi-rira, Otende nnyo Ka-baka wo;
Bamalayika ka- ba-yimbe, Ensi edde-mu, "A-miina"
