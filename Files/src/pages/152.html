CH 382
Ka Nkusemberere
Nearer, My God to Thee
4
4
Lowell Mason
Doh is G 
1. Ka nkusemberere (O)mulokozi!
Oba musaalaba, Ka- ngwetikke!
Oluyimba lwange, Kusemberera Ggwe;
Ka nkusemberere, (O)mu-lokozi.
	
2. Ng'emmomboze kunsi, Buwungedde,
Sirina wendaba Kiwummulo;
Okusaba kwange, Ku kusemberera
Ka nkusemberere, (O)mu-lokozi.

3. Ondag(e) ekkubo lyo, Mbeere omwo,
Yonna gy'onoontwala, Mp(a) e-kisa kyo;
Mu ntambula yange, Nkusembererenga,
Ka nkusemberere, (O)mu-lokozi.

4. Ku nkya nga nzuukuse, Nkuyimbire;
Mu bizibu byange, Nte-ndereze,
Nkusemberere nnyo, Mu mbeer(a) ey'ennaku,
Ka nkusemberere, (O)mu-lokozi.

5. Bw'olijj(a) okuntwala, Eyo gy'oli;
Ndiyita mu bbanga, Nga-nsanyuka,
Oluyimba lwange, Kukusemberera,
Ka nkusemberere, (O)mu-lokozi.
