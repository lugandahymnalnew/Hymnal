Kye Ndowooza Kyokka
One Sweetly Solemn Thought
An
6
8
Philip Philips
Doh is Bb 
1. Kye ndowoza- kyokka, Ge maka mu- ggulu,
Neyongedd(e) o-kusembera, Nnaate-r(a) okutuuka.

Ndi kumpi n'a-maka gaffe,
Kumpi nnyo, ku-mpi nnyo leero;
Nnaate-r(a) okutuuka.

2. Nsemberedd(e) e-waffe, Mu mayumb(a) a-mangi,
Eyo Yesu- gy'afugira, ku nnya-nja ennungi.

3. Bwe ndituka- eyo, Ndyetikkula- byonna,
Ennaku ndi-gyerabira, Ndyamba-la engule.
