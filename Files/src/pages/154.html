Netaaga Omulokozi
I Must Have the Saviour With Me
An
3 4
4 4
Jno R Sweney
Doh is Bb 
1. Netaaga Omulokozi, Kuba nze ndi munafu;
Netaga okuba naye, Ng'ankutte ku mukono.

Mu byonna nnaagumanga, Antwale gyayagala,
Nnaagenda seemulugunya, Bwenkulemberwa Yesu.

2. Netaaga Omulokozi, Siyinza kuwangula,
Wabula ye bw'anang'umya, Neddoboozi ly(e) eddungi.

3. Netaaga Omulokozi, Mu kutambula kwange,
Mu buzibu bwange bwonna, N'ebikemo by'omubi.

4. Netaaga Omulokozi, Andagenga ekkubo,
Amponye mu ntalo zange, N'okuwangula byonna.
