Kiseera Ki Ekisigadde?
How Far From Home?
An
4

Arranged
Doh is Eb 
1. Kiseera ki (e)kisi-gadde, Tutuke mu maka gaffe?
Ekiro kyonna ki-ggwawo Enkya esembera.
Totya naye dduka mangu Laba emmambya esala
Tunaatera oku-tuka (E)kiro gye kitaba.

2. Mbuza mulwanyi mu-nange, Naye nanziramu bwati,
"Ggwe nyweza nnyo eki-tala (O)lutalo terulwe.
Kale totya, naye guma, Omal(e) omulimu gwonna;
Lab(a) olutalo bwe- luggwa, Tulifun(a) engule".

3. Mbuuz(a) ebitond(e) eby'o-mu nsi, Nebigambira wamu nti.
"Ebiro biri ku-mpi nnyo, Ensi empya ejje.
Kale guma nnyo tokaaba, Bingi biraga ebiro;
Buli kitonde ki-rinze (O)kuvuga kwe'ngombe?"

4. Kiseera ssi kine-ne nnyo, (O)mutambuze asanyuke
Ennaku ze nga zi-wedde (A) maziga gaggwewo.
Kale sanyuka nnyo nnyini, Tuli kumpi okutuka,
Tube nessanyu bu-lijjo Mu nju ya Kitaffe.
