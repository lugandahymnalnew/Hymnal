Kya Ssanyu Omutambuze
How Sweet Are the Tidings
An
4
4
Arranged
Doh is Eb 
(Ku bitundu ennyingo zonna zaatule - kikkirizibwa)
1. Kya ssanyu omutambuze okuwulira Ng'ali ewala mu malungu,
Nti "Omununuzi anaatera okujja, Akutuuse mu ns(i) ensuubize."

Alijja! Mmanyi- nti Yesu ajja, --- Akomawo ku nsi kuno.
Naffe aba - - -na be - - - Abakooy(e) ennyo,
Tulifuga- ne Ye--su.

2. Entana z'abantu baffe bonna abaafa, Ziribikkuka zonna lumu;
Abantu be bonna abeebaka mu kufa, Balivaayo mu ntaana zaabwe.

3. Tunaabanga wamu fenna mu mirembe gye, Nga tuyimba n'essanyu lingi;
Abanunule be baliva mu nsi yonna, Okusinza Kabaka Yesu.

4. Aleruya, ye wewawo, Aleruya nnyo! Bwe tuba mu ye tulituuka;
Tunviikire okutuusa lw'alikomawo, Tuweebw(e) engule zobulamu.
