CH 578
Nkwetaaga Ayi Mukama
Blessed Lord How I Need Thee
An
3
4
E.E.Belden
Doh is G 
1. Nkwetaaga Ayi Mukama, Nz(e) omunafu, (o)mwonoonyi;
Onkwatenga ku mukono, Ompenga ku maanyi go.

(O)lwa leero, buli (e)ssaawa, Nkwetaaga ayi Mukama!
(O)lwa leero, buli (e)ssaawa, Beera nange tondeka.

2. Nyambaza obuwombefu, Nze ajjudd(e) amalala;
Ntegeeza bwe nd(i) omunafu, Nsembeza kumpi gy'oli.

3. Mu Ggwe mbeera n'emirembe, Bwembeera nzekka nnemwa:
Nkuma mu lugendo lwange, Ggwe (e)ssuubi lyange lyokka.

4. Byonn(a) ebinanjijiranga, Mu ssanyu ne mu nnaku;
Ku Ggwe kwe nanywereranga, Ggwe kiwummulo kyange.
