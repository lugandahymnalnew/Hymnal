Olunaku Olulungi Lujja
The Golden Morning
An
4
4
S J Graham
Doh is G 
1.0-lunaku olulungi lujja, Yesu lw'alijja;
O-kutwala abaana be fenna, Mu maka gaffe.

(E)kitiibw(a) ekimasamasa N'amaanyi eby'ekitalo ennyo!
Birigoba enzikiza, Entaana ziribikkuka.

2. E-njiri ye egenda ebuna, Mu buli ggwanga,
Ye-su alyoke ajje mangu nnyo, (E)ngombe ng'evuze.

3. A-najja ne bamalayika be, Ng'amasamasa,
Ye-su ajja okututwala ffe, Eteri kufa.

4. A-bantu mu nsi bonna abafa, Tulibalaba,
E-ra (a)maziga gaffe gonna, Agasangule.
