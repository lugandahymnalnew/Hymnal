1. Yesu aliku'nganya aba-ntu (O)kulabika mu maaso ge;
Ffe tuliba tutya musa-ngo, Bwalituyit(a) okuwoza?

Aliteek(a) e'ngano mu ggwani-ka, Alisuul(a) ebisu-su-nku;
Tu-liba tutya mu musa-ngo Ku lunaku olukulu?

2. Tulimuwulira ng'aga-mba, "Muddu omwesigwa weebale?"
Oba ffe tulitya nobulu-mi, Nga tugobwa mu maaso ge?

3. Alisanyukira abaanam be, Ng'alaba be yanunula;
Alibambaza eby'omu ggu-lu, Balifukamira w'ali.

4. Ka tutunule ffe twekume-nga, Ettabaza nga zaaka nyo;
Awasa omugole bw'ali-jja, Twetegeke okugenda.

5. Ka tulowooze eby'omu ggu-lu, Tulindirir(e) ekiseera;
(Olugendo lwaffe bwerulikoma, Tulibeera mu maaso ge.
