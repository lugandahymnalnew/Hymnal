Sitwalibwa Bugagga
Lord I Care Not for Riches
An
3
4
Frank M Davis
Doh is Ab 
1. Sitwalibwa bugagga feeza oba zaabu,
Njagala bya mu ggulu Nokutuuka gy'oli;
Mu kitabo kyo Yesu Omuli amannya,
Mbulirako, Mukama, Mulimu eryange?

Mu kitabo omwo omuli amannya,
Ontegeeze, Mukama, mulimu eryange?

2. Ebibi byange bingi Binzitowerera,
Naye omusayi gwo Tegulemwa kibi,
Yesu Ggwe wasuubiza Mu Byawandikibwa
Ebibi byaffe byonna, (O)kubifuul(a) ebyeru.

3. Mu kibuga kyo Yesu, Kye watekateeka,
Nekitiibwa ekingi Ekimasamasa,
Ekibi gye kitajja, Kwonoona baana bo,
Okuli abakuumi, Nange wampandiika?
