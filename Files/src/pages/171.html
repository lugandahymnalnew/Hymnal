Ekiseera Ky'omusango
The Judgement Has Set
An
3
4
E E Belden
Doh is Eb 
1. Ekiseera (e)kyomusange kituuse, Ebitabo bibikkuddwa;
Tuliba tutya mu- maaso ge, Ka-tond(a) O-mulamuzi?

Tuliyimirira- tutya? Ku lunaku olu-kulu?
(A) wamu n'ebibi bya-ffe byonna, ---
O-banga-bisanguddwa?

2. Omusango gwatandika n'abafu, Naffe kati tukeberwa;
Ebitabo by oku-jjukiza, Bi-rike-berwa ddala.

3. Tuliba tutya mu kaseer(a) ako, Ng(a) ebibi byaffe birabwa?
Omusango nga gu-saliddwa, Te-riba- kujulira.

4. Omusayi gwa Yesu gwe gunaaza, Ebibi byaffe bingi nnyo;
Tetulina nsonga- okutya, Yeng(a) atu-wolereza.
