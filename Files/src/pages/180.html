Waliwo Ensi Ennungi
There's a Land That Is Fairer
An
4
4
J E Webster
Doh is G 
1. Waliwo ensi ennungi nnyo, (O)lwokukkiriza tugiraba;
Mükama atulindiridde, Okütusembeza ffe gy'ali.

Mu ssanyu jjereere, Tulisisinkana mu ggulu;
Mu ssanyu jjereere, Tulibeera n'Omulokozi.

2. Mu nsi eyo tunaayimbanga: Ennyimba zessanyu eringi,
Eyo tewaliba kukaaba, Tulibeera mu ddembe lyokka.

3. Byo Kitafi(e) owomu ggulu, Tunasuutang(a) obulungi bwe,
Olw'ekirabo kyokwagala, Era n'emikisa gyatuwa.
