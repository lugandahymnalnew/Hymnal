CS 206
Ndibeera Ku Mugga
We'll Tarry by the Living Waters
An
4
4
E E Belden
Doh is Eb 
1. Ndibeera ku mugg(a) omulamu, Ensul(o) ey obuwa;
Eyo Yesu gy'atulinze ffe, Okutwaniriza.

Tulibeera ku mugga 0-gwo, Ogw'amazzi amala-mu;
[Tulibeera ku mugg(a) ogwomuggulu; Ogwamazzi agaw(a) obulamu]
Tubeere ku mugga 0-gwo, Ensul(o) ey'obulamu.
[Tubeere ku mugg(a) ogwo bulijo]

2. Abakooye olwolugendo, Tuliwummulako;
Ku mazzi agamasamasa, Agaleet(a) essanyu.

3. Yesu ge mazzi amalamu, Nnywa oddem(u) amaanyi;
Jjang(u) ofune obulokozi, Owon(e) ennyonta yo.
