Okwagala Mu Maka
Love At Home

Arr from J H M McNaughton
Doh is Ab 
1. Okwagala we kuba tewaba nnaku;
Amaka ag'ekisa gajjul(a) essanyu;
Buli wantu nga ddembe,
Mboozi empomerevu,
Liba ssanyu jjerere, Mu maka gaffe.

(O)kwagala mu maka,
Liba ssanyu jjerere,
Mu maka gaffe.

2. Mu nju libeera ssanyu, Olw’okwagala,
Mu ggulu basanyuka, Olwokwagala;
Wonna wonna mu nnyumba,
Nga wajjudde essanyu,
(O)bulamu busanyusa, Mu maka gaffe.

3. Ne Yesu asanyuka, Nga twagalana,
Okukyaawa n’obuggya tebibeeerawo;
Maka agaagalana,
Gabeera ga muwendo;
Goolesa obulungi, Obwa Kitaffe.

4. Yesu nfuula owuwo, Mbe n’okwagala,
Er(a) olwa saddaka yo, nfuula omuggya:
Ggwe omponye mu bubi,
Ompummulize mu Ggwe,
Ompe omukisa gwo, Mu maka gaffe.
