Ggwe Okwagala Okunene
O Perfect Love
4
4
Joseph Barnby
Doh is Eb 
1. Ggwe Okwagala, Okusukkiridde,
Tufukamidde nga tukusaba,
Obaw(e) okwagal(a) okutakoma,
Abagole boogass(e) olwa leero.

2. Baw(e) obulamu obutuukiridde,
Obw’okwagala n’okukkiriza;
Bawe essuubi, Bagumiikirize,
N’obwesige Obutatya kabi.

3. Bawe essanyu, Mu nnaku ey'ensi,
Baw(e) emirembe, Mu buli mbeera;
Enkya ennungi, Bagisuubirenga,
Nti lumu olidd(a) okubanona.
