Ku Musalaba Yanfiirira
Down at the Cross
4
4
J.H.Stockton
Doh is Ab 
1. Ku musalaba yanfirira, Kwe yampeera obulokozi,
Omusayi gwe, gwe gunnaaza, Yebazibwenga.

Nebaza Yesu,-- Yebazibwenga;--
Ku musalaba yanfirira, Yebazibwenga.

2. Newuunya nnyo bwe yandokola, Ye Mukama abeera mu nze;
Nansembeza ku musalaba, Yebazibwenga.

3. Gwe ow'amaanyi olokola, Nsanyuka olw'okuba mu Ggwe;
Era mu Ggwe mwe ndokokera, Webazibwenga!

4. Jjangu eri Yes(u) ow'ekisa, Waayo omutima gwo leero;
Naaba mu mazzi gwe owone, Yebazibwenga.
