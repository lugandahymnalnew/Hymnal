CH 474
Olwazi Olw'edda N'edda
Rock of Ages
An
3
4
Thomas Hastings
Doh is Bb 
1. (O)lwazi lw’edda n’edda Ggwe, Olwanjatikira nze;
Omwo mwe nekweka nze, (O)musaayi gwe mwe gwava;
Ebibi binziyeeko, Nfuula omuwanguzi.

2. Emirimu gy'engalo, N’okufuba kw’omuntu,
N’amazig(a), agajj(a) ennyo, Emisana n'ekiro;
Byo tebiggyawo bibi, Ggwe wekka (O)mulokozi.

3. Sirina nze bulungi, Nkwesize Ggwe bwesizi;
Omwereere nnyambaza, Omunaku mp(a) ekisa;
Laba bwendi omubi, Onaaze, nkuweereze.

4. Bwe ndituuka mu ggulu, Nga nnina ebibala;
Bwe ndiraba Ggwe Yesu, Ne nsanyukira w'oli:
Ndibeera nga siyinza, Okwegulumizanga.
