Baibuli Ebikukkulirwe Ensi
An Open Bible for the World
4
4
Wm J Kirkpatrick
Doh is Bb 
1. Baibul(i) ebikkulirw(e) ensi! Kyekigendererwa kyaffe!
Amazima gabikkulwe, Gasaasaanye emikisa.

Kigambo kye-(Kya mukisa)
Kibunyise --(Musana gwe)
Ku lukalu ne ku nnyanja-
Abali mu kizikiza- Kibazze eri Katonda.

2. Wonna Baibuli wetuuka - - Eyas(a) amusana gwayo;
Egoba ekizikiza, N'amaany(i) agekibi gonna.

3. Erag(a) abantu Kitaffe - - Oy(o) ajjudde okwagala;
Aboonoonyi mu Adamu, Ebaw(a) essuubi mu Yesu.

4. Etegeeza ebya Yesu, - - Eyaf(a) atuw(e) obulamu;
Era bonn(a) abakkiriza, Baba baana ba Katonda.
