Doh is Ab 
1. Kyensaba Yesu mbeere wuwo, Nnung'amya, nnung'amya;
Nkole by’osiima so si byange Nkusab(a) onnyambe.

Mbe wuwo (Yesu), Mbe wuwo (Yesu);
Ekyo nze lyenkusaba,
Mbe wuwo (Yesu), Mbe wuwo(Yesu);
Mbe wuwo nzenna Yesu.

2. Eby'ensi byonna bigasaki, Wotali, wotali?
Olw’erinnya lyo ka mbireke, Ggwe byonna gyendi.

3. Ng'amasanyu g’ensi ngaleka, Nsembeza, nsembeza;
Bwemba woli mba nnina byonna, Yes(u) onsembeze.
