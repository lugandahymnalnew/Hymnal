Abakunguzi Banaava Wa?
Where Are the Reapers
An
4
4
George F Root
Doh is G 
1. Abakunguzi bali ludda wa, Baggye abantu mu ns(i) ey'ekibi?
N’ekigambo kye ekya mazima, (O)kutuus(a) omulimu nga guwedde.

Abakunguzi banaava wa? Abanaakola mu ssany(u) eringi
Abaneewayo, tukungaanye, Abantu be bave mu ns(i) embi?

2. Mu banoonye mu makubo gonna; Mulimu ababulidde omwo;
Munoonye wonna mu buli kifo, Muleete bonna olw’ensi empya.

3. Ennimiro wonna zengedde nnyo, Ensi erinze amakungula;
Naye abakozi batono nnyo, Tunafiirwa bingi bwe tulwawo.

4. Mujje mw(e) abantu be nebiwabyo, Tukungulire wam(u) abantu be;
Tugumiikiriz(e) omulimu, Tukungulire mu ssany(u) eringi.
