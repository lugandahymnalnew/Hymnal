Tompitako Omulokozi
Pass Me Not, O Gentle Saviour
4
4
W.H.Doane
Doh is Ab 
1. Tompitako (O)mulokozi, Nkwegayirira;
Nga oyita abalala, Nange ompite.

Ayi Ye-su, Nsab(a) ompulire,
Nga bw'oyita abalala, Nange ompite.

2. Ku ntebe yo ey'ekisa, Mpebw(e) emirembe,
Nfukamidde wooli Yesu, Nsab(a) onsonyiwe.

3. Nkwesiga Ggwe Yesu wekka, Ndag(a) amaaso go;
Ompony(e) endwadde z'omwoyo, Yes(u) ondokole.

4. Ggwe nsulo y'essanyu lyaffe, Osinga byonna;
Tewali mulala ku nsi, Oba mu ggulu.
