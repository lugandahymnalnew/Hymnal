SDAH 511
Mmanyi Gwenakkiriza
I Know Whom I have Believed
4
4
James Macgranaham
Doh is Eb 
1. Simanyi lwa-ki- Katonda Yanjagala- bwa-tyo,
Nz(e) omubi a-ta-sanira Nangu-la mbe- wu-we.

Nze mmany(i) oyo, Gwennakkiriza,
Era mmanyi nti afaayo gyendi,
Akuuma byeyasuubiza;
Okutuusa iwalidda.

2. Simanyi lwa-ki- yampa nze (E)kis(a) ekindo-ko-la,
(O)Kukkiriz(a) e-ki-gambo kye, Kwampa emi-re-mbe.

3. Simanyi (O)mwo-yo- bwakola Ng'alung'amy(a) a-ba-ntu,
Nga babikku-li-ra Yesu, Nebamukki-ri-za.

4. Simanyi bi-nni-ndiridde - Bib(i) oba bi-ru-ngi;
(O)luvannyumalwo-kukoowa, Ndirab(a) amaa-so- ge.

5. Simayi Ye-su- lwalidda, Kir(o) o-ba mi-sana,
Katambule na-ye wano Era ne mu-ns(i) e-mpya.
