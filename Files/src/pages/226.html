SDAH 250
Mukama Omulokozi
He Hideth My Soul
An
6
8
Wm J Kirkpatrick
Doh is C 
1. Yesu ye Mulokoz(i) ow’ekitalo, (O)mulokoz(i) eyewuunyisa;
Akwek(a) obulamu bwange mulwazi, Mwendab(a) emigg(a) egy’essanyu.

Akwek(a) obulamu bwange mu Lwazi;
(E)kisiikirize mu ddungu,
Ankweka mu kwagala kwe okungi
(N’ankuuma n’omukono gwe.) x2

2. Yesu ye mulokoz(i) ow’ekitalo, Aggyaw(o) omugugu gwange;
Ampanirira n’esinyenyezebwa, Ampa amaanyi bulijo.

3. Ampa emikisa buli kiseera, Anzijuz(a) ekitiibwa kye;
Ndiyimba nga nzuukidde mu kitiibwa, Ku Mununuz(i) omwagalwa.

4. Bwendyambazibwa obutukuvu bwe, Mmusisinkane ku bire;
Ndiyimba n'abanunule abangi, Ku bulokoz(i) obwekisa.
