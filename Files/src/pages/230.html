Adopted by L L Picket
Doh is Eb 
1. Yogera nange Yesu mu ddobooz(i) eddungi,
Mpulire ng'ong'amba nti, "Guma toli wekka.”
Nnongoosa omutima mpulir(e) eddoboozi,
Nyimbeng(a) amatendo go, Nsanyukire mu Ggwe.

Ontegeeze n'ekisa, Mu kisa ky(o) ondage
Nti mu Gggwe ndiwangula, Ndifuna eddembe,
Ontegeeze kakano, Mu kisa kyo (e)kingi;
Mpulire bw'ong'amba nti “Guma toli wekka”

2. Tegeez(a) ababo bonna Balag(e) ekkubo lyo;
Obajjuz(e) essanyu lyo Bayig(e) okusaba.
Bawengay(o) obulamu Bwabwe bonna gy'oli,
Bakol(e) omulimu gwo (O)kutuusa lw'olijja.

3. Mukama mbikkulira Nga banabbi (a)b’edda,
Kye nsana okukola Ntuuse (a)mateeka go;
Ka nkugulumizenga, Nate ka nnyimbenga,
Nkolenga by'oyagala, Nkutenderezenga.
