Kristo Oba Balabba
Christ or Barabas?

F E Belden
Doh is C 
1. Nakola ntya Yes(u) ono, (O)mulokozi wange?
Ayambadde (e)fulungu N,engule (e)y'amaggwa?

(E)kibuuzo kye kino, (E)ky'okuddamu kiki:
Nkomerere Yesu Kri-sto, Balabba ateebwe?

2. Nnaalek(a) omubb(i) agende, Omunt(u) owekibi?
Oba Krist(o) Oyo Yesu, atalina kibi?

3. Kibuuzo nga kikulu! Kizibu nnyo ddala!
Ekisakye nga kingi, Nzenna kinkankanya!
4. (E)nkomerero ng’etuuse Ens(i) ery'egaan(a) etya?
(E)bigambo nti, "Twala eri, Twal(a) okomerere!"

5. (O)mukristayo wulira, era n’omwonoonyi,
Ekibuuz(o) eky'entiisa, onokiddam(u) otya?

(E)Kibuuzo kye kino, (E)ky'okuddamu kino:
Kankomerere Balabba, Yesu Krist(o) ateebwe.
