CH 183
Ettabaaza Zammwe Zaake
Let Every Lamp Be Burning Bright
An
3
4
F E Belden
Doh is E 
1. Ttabaaza zammwe zaake nnyo, E-kiro kisembedde;
Eky'enzikiz(a) ekutt(e) ennyo, Ye-su nga akomawo.

Twetegek(e) ab’oluganda,
Ttabaaza zaffe zijjule;
Mu-kama waffe wuuy(o) ajja,
Tu-koleez(e) ettabaaza.

2. Wadde bangi tebafaayo, Ku- bya kudda kwa Yesu;
Okudda kwe kukakafu, E-ryo ly'essuubi lyaffe.

3. (A)mazima g’ekigambo kye, Ye-tabaaza gye tuli;
Ne bwetwetoloolw(a) akabi, Ye-su abeera naffe.

4. (O)kukkiriza n’ebikolwa, Bi-rabikenga mu ffe;
Ggwe kkiriz(a) ekigambo kye, Ye-su abeere naawe.
