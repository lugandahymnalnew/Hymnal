Erinnya Lya Yesu Ddungi
How Sweet the Name of Jesus Sounds
An
4
4
A.R.Reinnagle
Doh is Eb 
1. Erinnya lya Yesu ddungi, Eri akkiriza!
Lisangul(a) amaziga ge, Limalamu (o)kutya.

2. Litereza n'omwoyo gwe, Linyig(a) ebiwundu;
Ye mmere yomuyal(a) era, Liwummuz(a) akooye.

3. Erinny(a) egganzi, lwe Lwazi, Bwe bwekweko bwange,
Lye ggwanik(a) eritaggwamu, (E)kisa (e)kirokola.

4. Yesu Omusumba wange, Ggwe Kabaka wange;
Ggwe bulamu bwange bwonna, Kkiriz(a) ettendo lyo.

5. Ndi munafu mu mutima, Nebyo byendowooza;
Bwenkulaba Ggwe nga bwo'li, Nkutendereza nnyo.
