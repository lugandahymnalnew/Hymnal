Katonda Musumba Wange
The Lord's My Shepherd
An
3
4
Jessie S Jrvine
Doh is F 
1. Katonda- Musu-mba wange, Era- Ye andiisa:
Nze ndi- wuwe, naye- wange; Byonn(a) e-byange bibye,

2. Andis(a) o-muddo- (o)mulungi, Nziku-ta, mpummula;
Awa-l(i) emigg(a) emi-rungi, Anywe-sa lwa kisa.

3. Bwe nkyama- Ye a-nkomyawo, N'ang'u-mya omwoyo;
So si-Iwa bulungi- bwange, Lwa li-nnya lye lyokka.

4. Mu kiwo-nvu e-kyo kufa, Ndiyi-ta nga sitya;
Olu-gga lwe n’omu-ggo gwe, Bye bi-nankuumanga.

5. (O)kwagala- kwe o-kulungi, Kunaa-banga nange;
N’oku-tendereza- kwange, Kwo te-kulikoma.
