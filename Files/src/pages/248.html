Yita Abalwanyi
Sound the Battle Cry
4

Wm F Sherwin
Doh is Bb 
1. Yit[a] abalwanyi, Lab[a] omulabe!
Lag[a] ebbendera ya Ye-su;
Mwesibe byonna, munywere mwenna,
Mulwanirir[e] Ekigambo kye.

Mu - zuu - ku - ke, Mukungaane mwenna,
Mwetegeke n’Ekigambo kye;
Mu - ge - nde Nga mwogera "Ozaana!"
Kristo ye Muduumizi waffe.

2. Ka tutambule, tumwolekere,
Tuteekwa okuwangu-la;
[E]ngabo, [e]bbendera, bimasemase;
Amazima ge tegalemwa.

3. Katonda waffe, otuwulire,
Otuyambe n’e kisa- kyo,
[O]lutalo luggwe nga tuwangudde,
Twambazibw[e] engule mu ggulu!
