Omusalaba Lye Kkubo
The Way of the Cross Leads Home
An
4
4
Wm F Sherwin
Doh is Ab 
1. (O)musalaba kkub(o) erintwala eka, Tewali kkubo ddala;
Siyinza nze kutuuka mu ggulu, Bwensubwa omusalaba.

(O)mu-salaba lye kkubo (lye kkubo),
E-ritutuusa eka (eka);
Kinsanyusa nga ntambula ng'enda,
(O)musalaba lye kkubo.

2. Lye kkubo omuli omusaayi gwe, Mukama mweyayita;
Singa mpit(a) omwo ntuuka muggulu, Mberenga wamu ne Yesu.

3. Ekkubo ly'e nsi leero nze ndikyaye, Sitambulire mu lyo;
Yesu ampita tugende eka, Annindiridde ku luggi.
