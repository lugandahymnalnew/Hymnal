CH 276
Ggwe Yesu Omulokozi
My Jesus I Love Thee
4
4
Adoniram J.Gordon
Doh is F 
1. Gwe Yesu Omu-lokozi, nkwagala,
Ebyonoono bya-nge byonna mbikyaye;
Ggwe wekka omwa-galwa wa-nge Yesu,-
Mukwano gwange- Yesu, nkwa-gala nnyo.

2. Kuba ggwe wasoo-ka, nange nkwagala,
Wangula n'omu-saayi gwo bwe wafa;
Ku lw'engule (e)y'a-maggwa ka-nkwagale,-
Mukwano gwange- Yesu, nkwa-gala nnyo.

3. Mu ssanyu ne mu- nnaku, nze nkwagala,
Nasuutanga e-rinnya lyo bulijjo;
(O)kufa nga kuse-mbedde ndyo-gera nti:-
Mukwano gwange- Yesu nkwa-gala nnyo.

4. Bwe ndiba nga ntu-se eyo mu ggulu,
Ndikusinza no-kwagala okungi;
N'okuyimba ndi-yimba n'e-ssanyu nti:-
Mukwano gwange- Yesu nkwa-gala nnyo.
