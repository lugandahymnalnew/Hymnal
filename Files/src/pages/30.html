CH 401
Yesu Gwe Eyanjagala
Jesus Lover of My Soul
6
8
Simeon B.Match
Doh is F 
1. Yesu Ggwe wanjagala Ka nzirukire gy'oli;
Amayengo g'ennaku Nga gankubira ddala.
Nkweeka mu mikono gyo, Abannumba bamaanyi,
Sirina magezi nze, Kkiriz(a) omwoyo gwange.

2. Tewali kiddukiro Wabula mu mwoyo gwo;
Tondekanga! Nteetera, Naatera kugwa bugwi
Essanyu lyange lyonna Ndiggya mu kubeerwa kwo,
Nsensenz(e) omutwe gwange
Wansi w'emikono gyo.

3. Ggwe wekka ggwe netaaga, Tewali akwenkana:
Agudd(e) omuyimuse, Vumula azirise.
Omulwadd(e) omuwonye, Zibula eyazibiddwa,
Nze njijjudde ekibi, Gw'oli butuukirivu.

4. Yaza gyend(i) ekisa kyo, Sangul(a) ebibi byange,
Nin(a) ennyont(a), ondetere (A)mazzi agampony(a) ennyo.
Gwe nsulo y'obulamu, Kansene omwo mwokka,
(O)mwoyo gwange gujjule (A)mazzi ag obulamu.
