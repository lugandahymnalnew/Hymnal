Ka Nnyimbe ku Kwagala
I Will Sing of Jesus' Love
4
4
E.E.Belden
Doh is Bb 
1. Ka nnyimbe ku kwagala
Kwa Yesu (e)yanjagala:
Eyava mu ggulu lye,
Okufa ku lwange nze!

Ka nnyimb(e) okwa-gala kwe, 
Nnaakute-nde-rezanga
Eyatti-bwa ku lwange,
Nneebaza eki-sa kye.

2. Yesu ye yanjagala
Nze nga si-nnamwagala,
Nga sinna-mukowoola
Yeyasook(a) okunoonya.

3. Okwaga-la kwo Yesu,
Tekute-geerekeka:
Nz(e) atali-na kalungi,
Lwaki wanjagala nze?

4. Yesu ye-raga gyendi,
Siyinza kumugaana
Okwaga-la kwe gyendi
Nange kumpaliriza.
