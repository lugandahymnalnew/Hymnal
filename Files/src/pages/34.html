Ontegeeze ku bya Yesu
Tell Me the Story of Jesus
4
4
J.R.Sweney
Doh is Eb 
1. Ontegeeze ku bya Yesu, Mbinywereze ku mwoyo;
Ebyo bising(a) obulungi Byonn(a) eby'omunsi muno,
Bamalayika baayimba Yesu bwe yazaalibwa;
Katond(a) aweebwe (e)kitibwa, (E)mirembe gi-be mu nsi.-

Ontegeeze ku bya Yesu, Mbinywereze ku mwoyo;
Ebyo bising(a) obulungi, Byonn(a) eby'omu nsi muno.

2. Bwe yabeera mu lukoola, Mu nnaku ezo zonna;
Bwe yakemebwa Setaani, Era bwe yawangula,
Mbulir(a) ebyomulimu gwe, Nebyennaku ze zonna;
Bwe yabonyabonyezebwa N'anyomebwa- abantu.-

3. Mbulir(a) eby'omusalaba, Bwe bamukomerera;
Ne bamuziika mu ntaana, N'azukira mu bafu.
Okwagala kwe (o)kunene, Tunakwenkanya na ki?
Kale ka nnebaze Yesu, Mukama ya-nnunula.-
