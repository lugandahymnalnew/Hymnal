Doh is A 
1. Nsanyuka okuyimba nti, "Yesu Yannunul(a) olwokufa kwe,"
Mu kwagala kwe yannunula, Ndi wuw(e) emirembe gyonna.

Yannunula, Yannunul(a) olw'okufa kwe,
Yannu-nula,- --- Ndibeera naye bulijjo.

2. Yannunula nsanyuka mu Ye, Essanyu lyange lingi nnyo,
Nga ndaba nti omusana gwe, Gunabanga munda yange.

3. Buli kaseera konna konna, Ndowooza ku Mulokozi,
Era olw'essanyu nennyimba, Ku kwagala kwe okungi.

4. Lunaku lwe bwe lulituuka, Ndiraba Kabaka wange,
Ankuuma mu lugendo lwange, Asanyus(a) omwoyo gwange.

5. Manyi ng'antegekedd(e) engule, Mu nnyumba y(e) ey'omu ggulu,
Bw'alimala okunnongoosa Ndibeera naye bulijjo.
