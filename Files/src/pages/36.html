CH 310
Nalyoka Ne Nkusenga
O Happy Day
An
3
2
E.F.Rimbault
Doh is G 
1. Nalyoka ne nkusenga Ggwe, Omulokozi Katonda;
Kye nvudde nsanyuka nnyini, Ne njatula bwe nsanyuse.

Nsanyuse, nsanyuse; Yesu yannazaak(o) ebibi;
Y(e) anjagaza by'ayagala; Ansanyus(a) ennaku zonna;
Nsanyuse, nsanyuse, Yesu yannazaak(o) ebibi.

2. Namusenga n'atangoba; Nze ndi wuwe naye wange;
Yampita ne nditegeera, (E)ddoboozi lye nga lya Yesu.

3. Edda nasagasagana, Kakano nteredde ku Ye;
Sikyamusenguk(a) alina, Mukama y'alina byonna.

4. Nalayira nti ndi wuwe, Kankyogerenga bulijjo,
Era bw'olijja Mukama, Ndisanyuk(a) okub(a) owuwo.
