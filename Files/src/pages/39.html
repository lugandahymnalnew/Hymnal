Emikisa Gyo Mukama
Lord I Hear of Showers
6

G.F.ROOT
Doh is G 
1. Emikisa gyo Mukama, Mpulidde ogigaba
Abalala bagifuna, Nange ka ginjiikeko.

Ndi wano, ndi wano,
Nange ka ginjiikeko.

2. Yesu Mukama tonsubya, Laba bwe ndi omubi,
Naye omponye mu byonna, Ka mpebwe ku kisa kyo.

3. Weewawo nkusobezza nnyo, Era nkunakuwaza,
Nga ngoberera ebyensi, Naye nsab(a) onsonyiwe.

4. Tonvako ayi Katonda, Ggw(e) otuwa okumanya,
Ndag(a) ekisa kya Katonda, Ompe emirembe gyo.
