CH 212
Jangu Ggwe Omwoyo
Hover O'er, Holy Spirit
6
4
JNO.R.SWENEY
Doh is G 
1. Jangu Ggw(e) Omwoyo wa Yesu, Otuule mu nze leero,
Ontukuze nze omubi, Jangu mu nz(e) olwa leero.

Beera mu nze leero, Ofug(e) omwoyo gwange,
Onjijuze amaanyi go, Jangu ayi Mukama.

2. Ggw(e) ojjuza omwoyo gwange, Siyinza kukyolesa,
Naye netaaga nnyo ojje, Jangu mu nz(e) olwa leero.

3. Ayi Yesu ndi munafu, Naye nvunnamye woli
Tuma Omwoyo wo ajje, Anjijjuz(e) obulamu

4. Onnongoose, ondokole, Onnaaze omutima,
Ggwe aggyako bonn(a) ebibi Jangu mu nz(e) olwa leero.
