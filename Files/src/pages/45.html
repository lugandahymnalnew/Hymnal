CH 612
Enjuba Eyaka Ku Ntikko
There is Sunlight on the Hilltop
An
3
4
M.T.Haughey
Doh is A 
1. (E)njuba eyaka ku ntikko, Ku nsozi ne ku nnyanja;
Omusana gulengerwa, Eri ku lubalama.
Naye omusana mungi, Gwaka mu mwoyo gwange,
Kuba Yesu ali mu nze, Aleeta enjuba ye.

Gwe omusana omulungi, Ogwaka munda yange;
Yesu Ggwe omusanyusa, Ggwe enjuba yange nze.

2. Mu nfuufu nze mwe ndireka, Ennaku zange zonna,
Nga onyambazizz(a) essanyu, Nettendo lyo eringi,
Mu maka agekitiibwa, Gewannongooseza nze,
Ndigenda nga njijjudde nnyo, Ekitiibwa kyo munda.

3. Mukama Ggwe eyantonda, Ndi wuwo okuv(a) edda;
Ettabaza yo gye wampa, Eyakenga bulijjo;
Mu maka ag'ekitiibwa, Gewannongooseza nze,
Ndigenda nga njijudde nnyo, Essanyu mu mutima.
