CS 301
Nkwagala Ggwe Ayi Yesu
I Love Thee Better, Lord
An
4
4
R E Hudson
Doh is C 
1. Nkwagala Ggwe ayi Yesu, Okusinga byonna!
Kubanga wampa eddembe, Erising(a) ery'e-nsi.

Nga tekutegerekeka, (kwagala),
(O)kwagala kw'olina;
Era nga tegutendeka (musaayi),
(O)musaayi gwo Yesu! (musaa-yi gwo!)

2. Manyi ng'oli kumpi nange, Okusinga ensi;
Ebigambo byo biwonya, Obulumi mu-nda.

3. Wateeka essanyu mu nze, Ka nsanyukenga nnyo;
Singa tewa'ngumya (o)mwoyo, Nandiraby(e) enna-ku.

4. Ayi Mulokozi wange, Eyo ndibeera ntya!
Obanga essanyu lyompa, Linsanyusa ku- nsi?
