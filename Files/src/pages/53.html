1. Nsanyuka okwoge-ra Ku by eri muggulu, 
Ku kitiibwa kya Yesu E-ra bw'atwa-gala; 
Njagal(a) okubyogera Kuba mbikaka-sa, 
Bimpummuza omwoyo Okusinga byonna. 

Nsanyuk(a) okubinyumya, Nnaabyogerangak(o) ebyo;
Ebigamb(o) e-bikulu, (E)by'ekisa kya Yesu.

2. Nsanyuk(a) okubinyu-mya, Bya kitalo- ddala;
Bisinga byonna byonna Bye- tulowoo-zako. 
Nabinyumyanga ebyo Bikulu nnyo gye-ndi;
Nolw'ekyo nsanyuka nnyo, (O)kubyogera wonna. 

3. Binsanyusa nnyo nnyi-ni, Okubyoge-rako,
Buli lwe mbyogerako Nze- bimpoomera nnyo.
Nsanyuk(a) okubinyumya Bangi tebama-nyi,
(O)bulokozi bwa Yesu Mu Byawandiikibwa.

 4. Njagal(a) okubyoge-ra Er(i) ababi-manyi,
Ababyetaaga ennyo O-kubiwu-lira,
Bwendituuka mu ggulu, Oluyimba lwa-nge
Biriba bigamb(o) ebyo, Bye njagala ennyo
