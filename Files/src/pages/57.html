1. Tewewolereza, (O)mulimu munene,
Weewereyo ddala, Yes(u) akukozese
Ayita nekisa, Jjangu ompereze,
Weewereyo ddala, Olisanyusibwa

Kkiriza, (leero) Tewewolereza;
Kkiriza, (leero) Weewereyo ddala
Bw'onneworereza,
Onoddibwamu nti,
"Kale Tonjagala,
Ogobeddwa wendi."

2. Teweworereza, Yesu akuyita,
Oludde ng'ogaana, Kakano kkiriza
(E)nnimiro zengedde, (A)bakozi batono;
Kkiriza n'essanyu Okumuwereza.

3. Teweworereza, Okulwa kya kabi,
Ng'otenguwa Yesu, Oy(o) eyakwagala;
Mu kiseera kino, ng(a) ekisa wekiri,
Ngakuwolereza, Jjang(u) osonyiyibwe.
