CH 626
Nze Ndowooza ku Nsi
I am Thinking Today
An
4
4
John R Sweney
Doh is A 
1. Nze ndowooza ku nsi Eyo ennungi ennyo
Yesu gy'alitutwala fenna,
Olw'ekisa kye Oyo eyatwagala
Ndiba n'emmunyenye mu ngule!

Mu ngule yange mulibaamu (e)mmunyeenye
Obuddde obwo bwe bulikya?
Nga ntuuse mu bifo bir(i) eby'omu ggulu, ---
Ndiba n'emmunyenye mu ngule?

2. Mu maanyi ga Yesu ka nkole, ka nsabe,
Ka nfube okubuuliranga;
Emmunyenye ezo ziriba nnyingi nnyo,
Mu ssanyu lir(i) eritakoma.

3. Ndisanyuka nnyo bwe ndiraba ku Yesu
Nga nnyanjula bennabuulira;
Nasanyukanga mu maaso ge bwe ndiba
N'emmunyenye mu ngule yange.
