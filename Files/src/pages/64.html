Laya Eng'ma Omukuumi
Watchman, Blow the Gospel Trumpet
4

William J Kirkpatrick
Doh is Ab 
1. Lay(a) e'ngoma omukuumi, Labula buli muntu;
Abawulira e'ngoma Beenenye balokoke.

Lay(a) engoma omukuumi, Bunyisa mu nsi zonna;
[wulira] Yesu bwatyo bw'ayagala Labul(a) abantu bonna.

2. Laya ku buli kasozi Na buli wantu wonna;
Obulokozi butuuke Mu bizinga by'ennyanja.

3. Mu bibuga ne mu byalo Ne mu gabira gonna
Bakoleze ettabaza Mukama ajja mangu.

4. Abasibibwa Setani Banafuna eddembe;
Omulokoz(i) atuyita Nokwagala nti "Mujje".
