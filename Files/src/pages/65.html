Kale Eggye Yesu
Onward, Christian Soldiers
4
4
Arthur S Sullivan
Doh is E 
1. Kale (e)ggye lya Ye-su, mugolokoke, Mulabe Mukama akulembedde,
Mu balabe ba-ngi Yesu mugabe; Mukam(a) atuyi-ta;- tugende naye.

Kale ggye lya Ye--su,- mugolokoke,
Mulabe Mukama akulembe.

2. Tugende mu maa-so, ng'abalwanyi be; Tusinge Setaani n'amagezi ge,
Tukkirize Ye-su ye ngabo yaffe, N'ekitala kya-ffe- kye Kigambo kye.

3. Erinnya lya Ye-su bwe liwulirwa, (A)baddu ba Setaani bakankana nnyo,
Mmwe mugum(e) emyo-yo, ab'oluganda, Mu Mukama wa-ffe- tuliwangula.

4. Mu bayite ba-ngi bagende naffe, Balyoke babeere abasajja be,
Kal(e) aboluga-nda tugum(e) emyoyo, Omugabe wa-ffe- ye wa maanyi nnyo.
