Zuukuka Ggwe Mwoyo
Wake My Soul
3
4
Thomas Arne
Doh is G 
1. Zukuka gwe mwoyo gwange, Leeta amaanyi go;-
Engule Yesu gy'atuwa Yetaaga kwewaayo.

2. Ddoboozi lya Mununuzi Ngayita (a)baana be,-
Ye mugabi w'ebirabo Eri ggwe ne bonna.

3. Abajulirwa bangi nnyo Balaba by'okola,-
Byonna eby'edda bireke Ogende mu maaso.

4. Yesu Omwanjuzi waffe, Mu lugendo luno,-
Tube mu Ggwe, tuwangule, Tutuuke mu Ggulu.
