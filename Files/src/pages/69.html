CH 581
Essuubi Lyange Lizimbiddwa
My Hope is Built On Nothing Less
An
3
4
W B Bradbury
Doh is G 
1. (E)ssuubi lyange lizimbiddwa, Ku Yesu n'omusaayi gwe;
Sirina kirala kyonna, Neesigamye nze ku Yesu.

Ka 'ngumirenga ku Yesu
Byonn(a) eby'ensi butaliimu;
Byonn(a) eby'ensi butaliimu.

2. Byonn(a) eby'ensi ne bwe bijja, Ka ndowooz(e) eby'omu ggulu;
Mu bulumi ne mu nnaku, Tayinza kundeka nzekka.

3. Mazima ge ge ga'ngumya, Ne byonna bye yasuubiza,
Ne bwenfirw(a) ebyange byonna, Ka nneesigenga Ye yekka.

4. Ekiseera kiri kumpi, Ka nneeweeyo annongoose,
Annaze n'omusaayi gwe, Ampe n'akabonero ke.

5. Enkomerero ng'etuuuse, Ensange nga neetegese,
Nga nin(a) akabonero ke, Aka Mwoyo Mutukuvu.
