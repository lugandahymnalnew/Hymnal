Watch for the Time is Short

Doh is F 
1. Ggwe Omukri-sta-yo,- Tunula kaaka-no,
Laba esi-gadde,- Kaseera buseera
Tunula, Yes(u) a-jja,- (O)mubiri muna-fu,
(O)mulabe wa- maa-nyi,- Tunula, saba-nga,

Omu-krista-yo,
Tu-nu-la, -
E-kiro n'emi-sana,
Tunulanga, -
Tu-nu-la, Saba.

2. Leka kubo-ngoo-ta, So tobuusabuu-sa
Waliw(o) eki-ra-bo, Kye wasuubizibwa.
Kwe kuwummuli-ra- Eyo Yesu gy'a-li,
Eyakufii-ri-ra- Tunula, saba-nga.

3. Bw'olumbwa o-mu-bi, Yesu ye aku-wa,
Eby'okulwa-nyi-sa- Gwobanga wesiga
Lindirira Ye-su,- Ye gwe musana- gwo,
Kano ke ka-see-ra,- Tunula saba-nga.
