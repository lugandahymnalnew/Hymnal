Tunaazimba ku Lwaki Kristo
We'll Build on the Rock
An
4
4
E E Belden
Doh is Ab 
1. Tunazimba ku lwazi Kristo, Olwazi- olwo (o)lulamu;
Okubwatuka n'omu-yaga, Nga bizze tetulitya.

Ffe- tu - - nazimba, Ffe- tu - -nazimba,
(Tunazimba ku Lwaz(i) olugumu, Tunazimba ku Lwaz(i) olugumu)
Ffe tunazimba ku Lwazi lwaffe,
Yesu Kristo yekka.

2. (A)bamu bazimba ku mu-senyu, Ku birooto by'obugagga;
Abalala bazimba- (a)wabi, Ku musingi gw'ekibi!

3. Waliw(o) abazimba ku- nnyombo, Ku bitiibwa ku malala;
Naye ggwe nno zimba ku- Lwazi, Olugumu, ye Yesu.
