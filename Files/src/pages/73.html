Doh is Ab 
1. Yesu kino kye njagala, Omwoyo ng'ogugwo,
(O)mugonv(u) er(a) omuwombefu, Ogukukkiriza.

2. Er(a) omwoy(o) oguwulira (E)biragiro byonna;
Mukama mwonotuulanga Okugusanyusa.

3. Omwoyo ogunazibbwa (O)musayi gwa Yesu,
Ogutabeeramu kibi, Ogujjudd(e) ekisa.

4. (O)mwoyo ogwenkanidd(e) awo Ogumpe Katonda;
Otwal(e) omutima gwange, Onfugire ddala.
