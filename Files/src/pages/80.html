Doh is Bb 
1. Tuwe omuliro ogwo,
Ggwe wawa bajjajja baffe;
Ogwabawukanya mu nsi
Ogwabawang(a) obuzira.

2. Fuka ku ffe Omwoyo wo
Gwe wawa bajjajja baffe;
Era gwe wawa Paulo,
N'abunyisa Enjiri yo.

3. (A)maanyi ge wawa Eriya,
Kakati tugetaaga nnyo,
Musa yamasamasanga,
Yobu yagumikiriza.

4. Jjukir(a) ebiro eby'edda
Oze (o)buggy(a) omulimu gwo!
Tukuwadd(e) emyoyo gyaffe;
Fuka amaanyi go ku ffe.
