Ennaku Endala Ziwedde
Another Six Days Work
An
3
2
L Mason
Doh is Bb 
1. Ennak(u) endala ziwedde,
Ssabbiiti yaffe etuuse,
Oze buggy(a) omwoyo gwange
Ku lunaku lwo Mukama.

2. Tusinze Yes(u) ow'ekisa,
Eyatuwa olunaku;
Kwe yateeka emikisa,
Egisinga obulungi.

3. Twebaza Omununuzi,
N'eddoboozi ery'essanyu,
Atuwe okuwummula,
Kw'awummuza abantu be.

4. Lino ly'eddembe lya Yesu,
Lye tuweebwa ng'omusingo,
Ogw'ekiwummulo kiri,
Eky'essanyu ejjereere.
