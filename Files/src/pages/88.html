Webale Okutukuuma
New Every Morning

Samuel Webbe
Doh is Eb 
1. Webale okutukuuma, (O)kutuyisa mu nzikiza;
Kaakati nno nga bukedde, Fuka ku ffe- ekisa kyo.

2. Ku lunaku luno (o)luggya, Oziyize obucwano;
Ku baana abato nga ffe, (E)bikemo tu-biwangule.

3. Nga tukola emirimu, Eminene n'emitono,
Tuleme kwerowozaako, Okusinga- ku bannaffe.

4. Ka tufaanane nga Yesu, Eyasook(a) okutwagala;
Ow'ekitiibwa nettendo, Eyegyako- ekitiibwa.

5. Ffe bwe tuti bwe tusaba, Yesu (o)tuyambe twetikke;
(O)musalaba gwaffe leero, Era tuku-goberere.
