Yesu Mukama Wulira
Lord in the morning
An
2
4
Aaron William
Doh is F 
1. Yesu buli nkya wulira, Okusaba kwange,
Nze kwe nsaba mu linnya lyo, Nga nkowoola gy'oli.
	
2. Katond(a) oli Mutukuvu, Okyawa obubi!
Nze nsembera mu maaso go, Lwa kusasira kwo.

3. Omwoyo wo annu'ngamye, Mu mpenda zo zonna
Longosa buli kye nkola, Era kye ndowooza.

4. Bonn(a) abatya erinnya lyo, Olibawanguza,
Mukama Ggwe ngabo yaffe, Ggwe bwesige bwaffe.
