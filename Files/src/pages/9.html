Ggwe Eyannunula
My Maker and My King
An
4

Doh is D 
1. Ggwe eyannunula nebaz(a) ekisa kyo,
Mu Ggwe mwe mul(i) ensibuko y'emikisa gyonna,
Mu Ggwe mwe mul(i) ensibuko y'emikisa gyo nna,

2. Nze ndi kitonde kyo Ggwe (o)bulamu bwange,
Katonda wange, nsanidde okukusuutanga,
Katonda wange, nsanidde okukusuuta-nga.

3. Kale nnaakuwa ki anti byonna bibyo?
Mpayo omutima gwange, saddaka yomwavu,
Mpayo omutima gwange, saddaka yomwa~vu.

4. Nsaba ekisa kyo, kinjijuz(e) amaanyi,
Okwagala kwange kwonna kubeerenga kukwo,
Okwagala kwange kwonna kubeerenga ku-kwo.
