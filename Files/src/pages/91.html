Beera Nange
Abide with Me
4
4
William H Monk
Doh is Eb 
1. Beera nange obudde buzibye;
Enzikiz(a) ekwata: beera nange;
Sirina nze mubeezi mulala,
Gw'abeer(a) abanaku, nange, mbeera!

2. Obudde bwobulamu buziba;
Bwaaka katono ne buwungeera:
Byonn (a) eby'ensi bidiba, bikyuka:
Ggw(e) atadib(a) atakyuka tondeka!

3. Nkwetaga Ggw(e) emisana n'ekiro;
Setani ky'atya kubeerawo kwo;
Katonda wange, (E)bire bwe bijja,
(O)musana bwe gwa 'ka, beera nange!

4. Siriko kye ntya, Yesu nga wooli:
Beera nange! Ompanguz(e) ebibi:
Owomes(e) amaziga ne nnaku;
Entaana n'okufa tebintiise.
