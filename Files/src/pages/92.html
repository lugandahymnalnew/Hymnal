Nga Ffe Tutunuulira
Day is Dying in the West
4
4
W F Sherwin
Doh is Ab 
1. Nga ffe tutunulira, Eggulu litegeeza,
Ekitiibwa kyo,- Yesu, Ngokolez(a) etta-baza ez'ekiro.

Oli Mutukuvu, Ayi Mukama! Ojjudd(e) eggulu n'ensi,
Bonna batendereza, Ayi Yesu!

2. Kiseera ky'enzikiza, Kitwetoolodde, Yesu;
Emmunyeenye zi-bikka Amaaso go Ka-tonda, Tuyimuse.

3. Mukama w'obulamu, Waggulu w(e) emmunyeenye,
Tuwambatire- ffenna, N'emikono gyo,- Yesu, Tukwagala.

4. (E)mmunyeenye bwe zigenda, Er(a) enjuba n'omwezi,
(E)mmambya esale ku nsi, Abantu tuzuu-kuke, Tukulabe.
