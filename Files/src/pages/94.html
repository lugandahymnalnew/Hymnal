Far and Near the Fields

Doh is Ab 
1. Ennimi-ro zo zenge-dde, Ebikungulwa- bingi,
Biri bu-li wantu wo-nna, Mu biwo-nvu, mu nsenyi.

Omukunguz(i) omuku-lu,
Ggwe Mu-kama wa- byonna.
Tum(a) aba-ddu mu nnimi-ro,
Ekisee-ra kituuse.

2. Tuma ku- nkya nga bwaka-kya, Ne mu ttuntu to-leka,
Era n'e-njuba ng'egu-dde, Banoge- buli wantu.

3. Nawe Mu-kama gw'atu-ma, Ku'nganya ebi-bala,
(O)kutuusa- enkomere-ro, Olisa-nyuka mu ye.
