CH 33
Mujje Mwenna Abakooye
Come, Ye Sinners, Poor and Wretched
4
4
Sicilian Melody
Doh is Eb 
1. Mujje mwe-nna- abakoo-ye, N'okutega-na kw'ensi,
Yesu y(e) a-na-baaniri-za, N'okusaasi-r(a) okungi;
Mu-jje- mwenna, Mu-jje- mwenna, A-ba-yi-ta- temutya.

2. Temutya- ne-wakuba-dde, Ng'ebibi bya-mmwe bingi;
Kyaba-kuu-tira kye- kino; Mweneny(e) a-ba-sonyiwe;
O-mwo-yo gwe, O-mwo-yo gwe, Ye- ana-ba-tukuza.

3. Lab(a) Omwa-na- wa Kato-nda, Leer(o) abawo-lereza!
Munaawo-na lwa musaa-yi, Bwe mwesigi-ra ddala;
E-ki-sa kye, E-ki-sa kye, E-ki-ro-ko-la bonna.

4. Abatu-ku-vu be bo-nna, Bamusinza- n'essanyu,
Ab'omu- nsi- ne mu ggu-lu, Bayimb(a) ama-tendo ge;
A-le-ruya! A-le-ruya! Ye-su-O-mu-lokozi.
